Programs Offered

OB Montessori Center – Learning Pathway from Casa to Senior High School


Casa (Pre-School)

A nurturing Montessori environment for young learners focusing on independence, creativity, and social skills.

Elementary

Builds strong academic foundations in reading, writing, math, and critical thinking through hands-on learning.

Junior High School

Develops analytical skills, responsibility, and collaboration while preparing students for higher education.

Senior High School

Offers academic tracks that prepare students for college, careers, and real-world challenges.


Design of Classrooms

Come and discover how our classrooms, artistically enhanced by the design team of NAZARENO / LICHAUCO, support the philosophy and educational method of Dr. Maria Montessori.

Each classroom has materials and spaces that encourage independent learning, collaboration, creativity, and hands-on activities to foster an environment where students can thrive.
